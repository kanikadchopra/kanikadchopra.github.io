I am an undergraduate student majoring in Statistic at the University of Waterloo. Currently, I am the President of the Waterloo Data Science Club, a first-year Mentor with Math Society and an undergradate marker for STAT231.

I am an incoming data analyst at Wish. Previously, I have interned as a data scientist at Intact Data Lab , and Goldspot Discoveries Corp. . I am interested in a career in data science and analytics - I enjoy digging deep into data and finding insights.

In my free time, I like to embroider, water color paint and cook. All of these activities are detail-oriented and require a lot of patience. I have found that, in addition to keeping my mind creative, these hobbies help me with my data science work. If you want to see some of my best work, feel free to message me!
