Kanika Chopra

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Statistics at the University of Waterloo. Currently, I am working as a Data Analyst Intern at Wish.

Previously, I have worked as a undergraduate marker for STAT231 at the University of Waterloo, interned as a data scientist at Intact Data Lab , and Goldspot Discoveries Corp. . I am interested in a career in data science and analytics - I enjoy digging deep into data and finding insights.

Mentorship and giving back to the community is really important to me. I am currently the External Affairs Coordinator for the Waterloo Data Science Club; in previous terms, I was the President, VP of Events and VP of Education. In addition, I have volunteered as a teen mentor with Big Brother Big Sisters for 5+ years, as a mentor with Tech+ and as well as a mentor to aid first year students with Math Society.

In my free time, I like to embroider, water color paint and cook. All of these activities are detail-oriented and require a lot of patience. I have found that, in addition to keeping my mind creative, these hobbies help me with my data science work. If you want to see some of my best work, feel free to message me!
