Kanika Chopra

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Statistics at the University of Waterloo. Currently, I am working as an undergraduate teaching assistant for CFM 101 - Introduction to Financial Markets and Data Analytics with Professor James R. Thompson. This is a new FinTech course being introduced in Fall 2021, and hence, I am involved with the planning and creating the assignments, solutions and tutorials for students.

With regards to my data experience, previously, I have worked as a Data Analyst Intern at Uber on the Safety & Insurance team, as a Data Analyst/Scientist Intern at Wish on the Data & Relevancy team, interned as a data scientist at Intact Data Lab , and Goldspot Discoveries Corporation .

As per academia experience, I have also worked as an undergraduate marker for STAT231 at the University of Waterloo - I am interested in a career in data science and analytics in the future.

Mentorship and giving back to the community is really important to me. I am currently the External Co-Director for Tech+, which involves overseeing our mentorship program, 16 weeks of internship youtube series, and any other external facing events and resources. In addition to this, I have previously been the President, VP of Events, VP of Education and External Events Coordinator for the Waterloo Data Science Club, With regards to mentoring programs, I have volunteered as a teen mentor with Big Brother Big Sisters for 5+ years, as a mentor with Tech+ to help students interested in data science and as well as a mentor to aid first year students with Math Society.

In my free time, I like to embroider, water color paint and cook. All of these activities are detail-oriented and require a lot of patience. I have found that, in addition to keeping my mind creative, these hobbies help me with my data science work. If you want to see some of my best work, feel free to message me!
